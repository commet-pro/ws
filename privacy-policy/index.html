1. Introduction

Welcome to Commet.pro. We operate as a premium B2B technology growth, AI, and performance studio. We are committed to protecting and respecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, process, secure, and manage your data when you visit our website or interact with us.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect data that is strictly necessary to communicate with you and provide our services:

  • Contact Information: First Name, Last Name, Email, and message contents when filled through our contact forms.
  • Communication Data: Phone number and metadata if you contact us directly via our WhatsApp Business link.
  • Analytical & Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and browsing behavior collected via Google Analytics and cookie technologies to monitor site performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to:

  • Respond to your project inquiries, requests, and questions.
  • Optimize our website, analytics, and service performance.
  • Deliver custom software integrations, campaigns, and automation architectures.
  • Fulfill legal and security obligations.

4. Data Storage and Security

We employ top-tier technical and organizational measures to safeguard your personal data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. Our database pipelines are securely encrypted, and static communications are conducted via secure HTTPS channels.

5. Your Rights (GDPR & LGPD)

Depending on your location, you have several rights regarding your personal data under the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the Brazilian General Personal Data Protection Law (LGPD):

  • Access & Correction: You can request a copy of the data we hold on you or ask to correct errors.
  • Erasure & Deletion ("Right to be Forgotten"): You can request that we delete your personal data from our active systems.
  • Portability: You can request the transfer of your data to another provider in a structured format.
  • Consent Withdrawal: You can withdraw your consent to data processing at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us directly at contact@commet.pro.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect regulatory shifts or changes in our operational procedures. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
